Attractions and Activities

Kedarnath

  • Kedarnath Temple: Spiritual pilgrimage site, sacred to Lord Shiva.
  • Trekking: Scenic trek through mountains and valleys to the temple.

Kanyakumari

  • Vivekananda Rock Memorial: Iconic monument with ocean views.
  • Sunrise & Sunset Views: Spectacular views at the meeting point of three oceans.

Jammu Kashmir

  • Dal Lake: Shikara rides and houseboats in Srinagar.
  • Gulmarg: Skiing and cable car rides in the Himalayan mountains.

Cameron Highlands

  • Tea Plantations: Tour lush tea estates and factories.
  • Strawberry Farms: Pick fresh strawberries and enjoy local produce

Bangkok

  • Grand Palace & Wat Phra Kaew: Explore the royal palace and the Emerald Buddha temple.
  • Floating Markets: Experience traditional Thai shopping and food on water.
